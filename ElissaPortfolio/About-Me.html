I’m Elissa DeBruyn, a social butterfly who likes to make jokes and explore new forms of media. I’m a recent graduate from Ithaca College with a degree in Integrated Marketing Communications. Over the last few years, I’ve worked on everything from ad campaigns and brand identities to event posters and social media posts. Along the way, I’ve learned how to create cohesive campaigns with compelling images and harmonious layouts. I approach each new creative problem like a riddle that’s tough to crack. I research the current situation to understand the problem, then I look at all the ways to solve it, knowing that it’s often an unexpected solution that leads to the best idea.


I’m fun-loving, ambitious and empathetic. In my spare time, you can find me reading, traveling, writing song parodies or finding the perfect gif. I love trying new things (read:food) so my dream is to star in those BuzzFeed taste test videos.
