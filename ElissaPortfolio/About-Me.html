Hey! I’m Elissa DeBruyn, a social butterfly who likes to make jokes and explore new forms of media.

I’m fun-loving, ambitious and empathetic. You can often find me reading, traveling, writing song parodies or finding the perfect gif. I love trying new things (read:food) so my dream is to star in those BuzzFeed taste test videos.

In typical six-year-old-me fashion, I wore socks that matched my outfit instead of each other to my cousin's wedding. He went on to found Little Miss Mismatched. Although I haven't created any nationally known companies myself, I still draw on that creativity today.

I’ve worked on everything from digital ad campaigns and brand identities to event posters and social media posts. Along the way, I’ve learned how to create cohesive campaigns with compelling images and dynamic layouts. I approach each new creative problem like a riddle that’s tough to crack.
